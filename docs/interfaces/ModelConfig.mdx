> `optional` **instructions**: `string`

***

> `optional` **max\_response\_output\_tokens**: `number` \| `"inf"`

***

> **modalities**: `string`[]

***

> **name**: `string`

***

### provider

> **provider**: `string`

Model provider (e.g., 'tiwater')

***

### temperature?

> `optional` **temperature**: `number`

Temperature for response generation

***

***
